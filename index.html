Connect Four:

Instructions: The objective of the game is to place your tokens in a row of four before your opponent. You will take turns placing one token at a time while preventing your opponent from getting four in a row. Your tokens can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal. The possibilities are endless! Press play to start the game.
