Connect Four:

Instructions: The objective of the game is to stack your tokens in a row of four before your opponent. You will take turns stacking one token at a time while preventing your opponent from getting four in a row. You must click where on the board you want your token to go. Remember, you token must either have another token it if it is not on the bottom row. Your winning streak can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal. The possibilities are endless! Press play to start the game.
