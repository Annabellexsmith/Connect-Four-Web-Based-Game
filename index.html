Connect Four:

Let's play Connect Four with a twist! Winter in Seattle has been gloomy and Spring is just around the corner. Whoever wins will determine if Seattle gets an early spring or a extended winter. Player One, you will have sun tokens. Player Two, you will have cloud tokens.


The objective of the game is to stack your tokens in a line of four before your opponent. You will take turns stacking one token at a time. You must click where on the board you want your token to go. Remember, your token must either have another token below it if it's not on the bottom row. Your line can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal. Press play to start the game.
